Horror and Thriller

Horror and thriller movies keep me interested because they build suspense and make me think. I like stories that feel intense and unpredictable, especially when the movie has strong atmosphere and sound design.

  • Us (horror/thriller)
  • Sinister (horror)
  • Sinners (thriller)

Fantasy and Musicals

Fantasy and musical movies feel imaginative and creative. I like when the visuals are bold, the characters are memorable, and the story feels larger than life.

  • Wicked (musical/fantasy)
  • Matilda (family/fantasy)

Drama and Coming-of-Age

Drama movies can be emotional and meaningful. I like stories that focus on relationships, growing up, and learning difficult lessons.

  • My Girl (drama/coming-of-age)

Genre Mood Board

Even though these genres are different, they all have something in common: strong storytelling. Whether a movie is scary, emotional, or imaginative, I enjoy films that make an impact.
