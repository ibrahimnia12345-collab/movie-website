Movies as Storytelling

“Movies touch our hearts and awaken our vision, and change the way we see things.”
— Martin Scorsese

I love movies because they tell stories in a way that feels immersive. A great film can build a world, introduce unforgettable characters, and create emotions that stay with you even after the credits roll.

What I Pay Attention To

  • Atmosphere: lighting, sound, and pacing that set the mood
  • Characters: personalities that feel real and memorable
  • Themes: a message that makes you think
  • Music: soundtracks that make scenes hit harder
  • Visual style: creative cinematography and color choices

How Movies Inspire Me

Movies also inspire my imagination. They make me think about my own creative ideas, the kinds of stories I want to write, and how different genres can create completely different feelings.

Closing Thoughts

Whether it’s horror like Us and Sinister, a classic like Matilda, or something emotional like My Girl, I enjoy movies that make an impact. The best films feel meaningful, creative, and unforgettable.
